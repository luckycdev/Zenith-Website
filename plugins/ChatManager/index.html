ChatManager

by

luckycdev

Customizable chat banned words & names filter, customizable chat access level prefixes and colors, and customizable join/leave messages

v...

Features:

  • Optional access level prefixes
  • Optional access level chat colors
  • Optional customizable join and leave messages
  • Optional banned words filter for chat
  • Hides player names that have a banned word in their name
  • Kicks players with a banned word in their name
  • Fully customizable (prefixes, colors, join/leave messages, bad word/name filter)
